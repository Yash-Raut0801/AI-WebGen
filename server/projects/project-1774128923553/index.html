About Us

AEC Civil Solutions is a leading civil engineering and construction firm specializing in the design, planning, and execution of complex infrastructure projects. With decades of experience and a team of highly skilled engineers and professionals, we are committed to delivering innovative, sustainable, and cost-effective solutions that meet the highest industry standards.

Our expertise spans across various sectors, including urban development, transportation, water management, and energy infrastructure. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, commitment to safety, and ability to tackle the most challenging engineering feats.
